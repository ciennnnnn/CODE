TRAPICO SYSTEM

Modernizing Traffic Enforcement & Dispatch

TRAPICO is a unified complaint and dispatch platform built for traffic enforcement agencies in Quezon City. We streamline complaint intake, officer coordination, and real-time case resolution all from a single, mission-critical interface.

Our platform connects civilians, dispatch officers, and field personnel in a shared operational picture reducing manual response and better accountability.

Our Mission

Integrated Dispatch
A single workflow for complaint intake, validation, assignment, and resolution eliminating siloed tools and radio-based coordination gaps.
Duplicate Detection
Automated flagging of overlapping complaints reduces redundant dispatches and ensures every incident is handled once, correctly.
Full Audit Trail
Every case action from submission to resolution is timestamped and logged, giving command staff complete visibility and accountability.
